About Us

Home / About Us

Building Excellence Since 2010

Apiaro Constructors and Supplies Company Limited has grown from a small local contractor to one of Uganda's most trusted construction companies. With over a decade of experience, we've built a reputation for quality, reliability, and integrity.

Our commitment to excellence has seen us complete over 150 projects across residential, commercial, and infrastructure sectors. We combine modern construction techniques with time-tested craftsmanship to deliver results that exceed expectations.

Our Mission

To deliver exceptional construction services and quality building materials that transform communities and create lasting value for our clients through innovation, integrity, and excellence.

Our Vision

To be the leading construction and building supplies company in East Africa, recognized for quality, innovation, and sustainable building practices that shape the future of infrastructure.
